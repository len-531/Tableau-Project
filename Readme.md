# Netflix Dashboard Analysis

## Abstract

This dashboard provides an overview of the Netflix library, highlights release year trends and rating distributions across movies and TV shows. 
The dataset used contains 8,807 titles , of which 6,131 are movies and 2,676 are TV shows. 
Movies make up the majority of the collection. The rating distribution shows that TV-MA and TV-14 are the most common content ratings.
Release year trends reveal significant growth in content production after 2010, with a peak around the year 2018–2019. 
Genre analysis shows that Drama, International Movies, and Documentaries are the most prevalent categories, followed by stand-up comedy and family-oriented content.
Overall, the dashboard offers valuable insights into the composition, audience targeting, and production trends of the Netflix library.
